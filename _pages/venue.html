Direction

From Nice Cote d'Azur airport

  • Reach bus stop Aéroport promenade
  • On Friday: Bus 630 and 632 (632 operates only in the morning until 9am). Get off at Saint Philippe in Biot.
  • On Saturday:
    • option 1) Bus 637 (only one trip at 9.15am). Get off at Saint Philippe in Biot.
    • option 2) Bus 620 Get off at Pôle d'échange (train station in Antibes) and take the buses below.

From Antibes train station

  • Reach bus stop Pôle d'échange .
  • Bus A or 12. Get off at Saint Philippe in Biot.
