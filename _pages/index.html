FrangiPh0wn - CTF Teaser - January 9, 16:00 - February 9, 2026 12:00 CET

FrangiPh0wn is the ultimate galette for hackers.

Prepared by your favorite Ph0wn staff, it's the perfect way to warm up for the upcoming Ph0wn CTF. Take on the challenges, and hunt for the trinkets. Only the most skilled will uncover all the secrets!
FrangiPh0wn is an online CTF. Players play individually, for glory ... and a few prizes.

Get ready! Head to ctf.ph0wn.org , register, and enjoy your hacker galette!

Ph0wn CTF 2026 "Flag Ship" will be on March 13-14, 2026:

Ph0wn (pronouced like phone) is a free Capture The Flag (CTF) and Workshop event dedicated to the Smart World. Anything "smart": from smart phones to embedded devices, IoT, Operational Technology...
We usually have challenges on protocols or customization of connected devices, Android, hardware, retro-gaming, AI, OSINT and programming for IoT/OT/embedded.
It's an on-site CTF: participants interact with equipment.
