## Ph0wn 2023 Edition
<p>
  Ph0wn 2023 took place on <b>November 24-25 2023</b>. There was <b>182</b> participants in <b>46 teams</b>.
</p>
<p>
<i>41 teams</i> scored at least once. 
  Teams coming from mostly France (Provence, Paris, Lyon, Grenoble, Rennes, Toulouse...), but also 3 teams from Switzerland. There were 35% of students, 65% of professionals. 
</p>
