- 2 Minitel-based challenges
- 1 challenge with a Schneider PLC M221

## Ph0wn2Own

Ph0wn 2026 featured a unique "ph0wn2own" event. Participants were given a few weeks in advance a firmware to hack into (VoIP phone). 

Victory Conditions:
<ul>
    <li>🏴‍ Obtain a <strong>root shell</strong> (the ultimate treasure!)</li>
    <li>💣 Demonstrate a fully working exploit live</li>
    <li>⏰ Be swift, be precise, be legendary</li>
</ul>

## Ph0wn Warm Up


This is a <b>social event</b> organized by <a href="https://shl.contact">Sophia Hack Lab "SHL"</a> <img src="/assets/images/sponsors/logo-SHL-dark.png" width="30px" />. 

The event was held at <b>Business Pole, NIDA</b>, Sophia Antipolis, organized by SHL and sponsored by <b>Bashroom</b>. 

Hackers trained on former CTF challenges, ate pizzas, and worked on old Amstrad computers.
