Ph0wn is a Capture The Flag (CTF) dedicated to smart devices

Come and challenge yourself on IoT, embedded systems, smart phones, e.g drones, IP web cameras, console games, smart toothbrushes and many other devices!

Ph0wn 2019 will be held on Friday, December 13th 2019


How to participate

What to bring, how to compete...

How to participate

Team

Team size is limited to 5

Laptop

Bring your own laptop, power cord, adapters, multi plug outlets, USB cables, chargers, SD card or any of your favorite tools

Software

Bring your favorite software (disassemblers, development tools, hexadecimal viewers, virtual machines, kali etc)

Wifi

Challenges will be accessible via wifi. Make sure you can access wifi on your laptop.

Venue

Campus SophiaTech

The event is located in the Learning Centre

Car

A8 motorway: Exit 44, Antibes, Sophia Antipolis. Follow Sophia Antipolis access from the roundabout at INRA, Route Chappes. Recommend parking: P2

Airport

From the airport of Nice: line 230 or "230 Express" will take you to Sophia Antipolis in approximately 40 minutes for only 1.50 euros. The bus isn't very well indicated from the airport, you might have to ask someone for directions. Basically, you have to cross "Promenade des Anglais" to get to the bus stop. This bus does not run on week-ends. Ask to stop in Sophia Antipolis at Templiers

Train

Stop at Antibes. Then use bus, taxis, Uber or car sharing up to Sophia Antipolis

Bus

Get off the bus stop "Templiers" - Access by "Route des Colles". From the neighboring towns of Sophia Antipolis : Envibus.From Grasse and its surroundings: Sillages. At 2am (end of the CTF), there are no longer any buses on Sophia Antipolis. You will need to call a taxi or Uber, or share a care with other participants.
